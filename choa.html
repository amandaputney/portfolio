Project Overview

This redesign effort was the result of a week-long Hackathon I participated in December 2023. I collaborated with a team of User Experience Designers and a team of Software Engineers to identify a non profit organization and redesign their website to optimize the experience of the user. The bulk of the software development happened over a period of 2 days, with 5 engineers. Not all of the links and buttons are active on the site due to time restraints. Our main focus was building out the home page and the separate service type pages.
