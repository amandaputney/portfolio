Forget Me Not

Forget Me Not is a food waste reduction application that was created in a collaborative, agile environment using Django, Python, & PostgreSQL. It is equipped with email reminders and photo-upload functionality to track receipts & purchases.

Live Link

Project Overview

This application was built in an agile environment in collaboration with DeAndre Minor & Paul Lee for the purpose of reducing food waste through an efficient, technology driven solution. This Full Stack CRUD application lets users track their persishable food items by uploading receipts, creating/editing/deleting perishable items, and creating/editing/deleting email reminders of expiration dates.
